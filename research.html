My research is on Gromov-Witten invariants and their applications to symplectic topology. In particular, I work on questions related to extrinsic topology/geometry of Lagrangian submanifolds, and symplectic/Lagrangian embedding problems. In the past, I have also thought about problems in Ergodic theory.
