Student Seminar on Symplectic Geometry

HU-student Seminar on Symplectic Geometry (summer semester 2021)

Organizers: Shah Faisal and Naageswaran Manikandan

We aim to cover the contents of Lectures on Symplectic Geometry by Ana Cannas da Silva. The seminar can act as a prerequisite to advanced symplectic events that happen at HU. Our talks happen virtually via Zoom on Friday from 1:00 pm to 3:00 pm during the summer semester 2021 in Berlin. This seminar is conducted for educational purposes and you won't get any credits for your master or bachelor's degree by attending this seminar.
