About me: I am a final year PhD student of Mathematics at the Berlin Mathematical School (BMS). My research supervisors are Prof. Klaus Mohnke and Prof. Kai Cieliebak. Here is my CV .

I will be joining IRMA as a postdoctoral researcher with Prof. Alexandru OANCEA under the project: COSY : New challenges in symplectic and contact topology.

Research Interests

My PhD project is about symplectic Gromov-Witten invariants and their applications to symplectic topology. In particular, I work on questions related to extrinsic topology/geometry of Lagrangian submanifolds, symplectic capacities, and symplectic/Lagrangian embedding problems. In the past, I have also thought about problems in Ergodic theory.
