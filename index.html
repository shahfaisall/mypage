About me

I am a final year PhD student of Mathematics at Humboldt University of Berlin, and a Phase-II student of Berlin Mathematical School (BMS). My research supervisors are Prof. Klaus Mohnke and Prof. Kai Cieliebak. Here is my CV .

In my PhD project, I study symplectic Gromov-Witten invariants and their applications to study Lagrangian submanifolds and symplectic/Lagrangian embedding problems.
